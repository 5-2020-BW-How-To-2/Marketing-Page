How To

We'll put all the little clouds in and let them dance around and have fun. That's a crooked tree. We'll send him to Washington. Didn't you know you had that much power? You can move mountains. You can do anything. A fan brush is a fantastic piece of equipment. Use it. Make friends with it. Let's build some happy little clouds up here. Let your imagination be your guide.

Don't be afraid to make these big decisions. Once you start, they sort of just make themselves. It takes dark in order to show light. See there how easy that is. A tree needs to be your friend if you're going to paint him. God gave you this gift of imagination. Use it.
